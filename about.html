Our Coaches

Christian Olanowski

Born and raised in Virginia Beach, VA, Christian was a 4 time state placer while winning a state title during his senior year. Follow his state title, he placed for the "Beast of the East" championship and was a honorable mention for the Ascics All-American Team. After high school, Christian attended the Michigan State Univeristy and was a part of the Spartan's Varsity Wrestling Team in the 133/141 lbs weight class. He was an active wrestler for the Spartans for 3 years until a series of injuries cut his career short. He was able to graduate with a B.S. in Advertising with a concentration in management. Christian is the founder and president of Prestige Worldwide Wrestling, a national wrestling team for high school athletes in Virginia. He continues to be an active person within the wrestling community, educating amatures and professionals alike.

Matt Akers

Matt wrestled at Ferrum College where he wrestled at the state and national level. In 2017, he was apart of the NWCA nationals duals 8th place team, the NCAA southeast conference/regional team championships, and the NCAA southeast wrestling conference place winner. As an experenced coach, Matt has been instructing at VATP, Hermitage Mills Godwin, and Deep Run High School. He is currently pursuing a degree in Exercise Science at Virginia Commonwealth Univeristy. While being a full time student, Matt brings his knowledge and skills to RiverCity Wrestling.

Corbin Allen

Corbin Allen attended and wrestled at the University of Virginia. At 160 lbs, he was ranked no.18 nationally by Flowrestling and no.19 by Intermat in high school. He is a 2 time state champion in class 4A and was runner up in the 2014 "Beast of the East" competition. He now works with RiverCity Wrestling to help the local wrestling community.

Our Facilty

Here at RiverCity Wrestling, we have a wide open space for all of our members and coaches to actively and safely use. At the entrance, we have our front desk where members can sign in and be greeted by coaches. Before entering the gym, we have a small changing area along with a restroom. Mats are present throughout the gym to insure a safe activity area. A small weight lifting area is located in the rear of the gym.
