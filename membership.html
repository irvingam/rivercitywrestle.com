Memberships

Pricing

Our memberships are paid on a monthly basis. Patrons can select what type of plan they want based on how many days they want to come to the gym each week. Our prices are as follows:

1 Day/Week: $60

2 Days/Week: $85

3 Days/Week: $110

Payment

Payment can be submitted through our PayPal link below.

Waivers

Before entering the gym, members will have to have all waiver forms filled out along with a valid ID. Parental signature will be required if member is a minor. All members will have to current/renewed USA wrestling card in order to wrestle. All policies are firm and there will be given no exceptions.
